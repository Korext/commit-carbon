# Governance

Commit Carbon methodology governance is distinct from other Korext projects because methodology changes have legal weight in sustainability reporting.

## Methodology Versioning

### Major Versions (1.0, 2.0)
- 90 day public comment period
- Required: review by at least 2 climate scientists, 2 AI researchers, 2 sustainability professionals
- Final approval by steering committee
- Historical data can be recomputed with new methodology or preserved with original

### Minor Versions (1.0, 1.1)
- 30 day public comment period
- Maintainer consensus required
- Non-breaking changes to factors or assumptions

### Factor Updates
- Updated quarterly
- Require source citation for every change
- No public comment period required
- Change log maintained in PEER_REVIEW.md

## Steering Committee

- 3 Korext representatives
- 2 community members (elected annually)
- 1 climate science advisor
- 1 regulatory expert advisor

Conflicts of interest are disclosed for all committee members.
